On the belt pack mics we use rechargeable batteries (NiMH), however, if using traditional or alkaline batteries:

| Green                          | Ready                                 |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| Solid Red                      | Low battery (less than one hour left) |
| Flashing red then shutting off | Batteries completely dead             |


Though even at solid red you don't really have an hour so don't let them get to that point.
