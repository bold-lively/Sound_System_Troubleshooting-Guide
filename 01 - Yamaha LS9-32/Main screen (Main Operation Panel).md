Things that we actually use:
 - Display access -  these change the menu screen, nothing there's really nothing you cam mess up here, to get back just keep hitting the home button.
 - Layer - Everything should be on layer 1 or channel (1 - 32)
- Selected Channel Section - Adjusts  Gain, Pan, Threshold level of Dynamics, EQ, and Send Level. Learning this stuff is important but never gets taught cause it isn't absolutely necessary
Everything else:
- Mix/Matrix - We flat out don't have the equipment to use this but in case you were wondering the Mix is meant to give you the ability to send certain channels/signals to stage monitors so they can hear themselves and crap like that. The Matrix takes the sound from everything and can either record it or send it to a secondary location
- Stereo Meter and cue Section -  Stereo meter has 32 segment LEDs.  The cue led lights when a channel’s cue is active. Any cue can be canceled immediately by pressing the "clear" button.
